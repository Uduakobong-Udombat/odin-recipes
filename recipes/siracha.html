Saucy Sriracha Franks

A little heat, a little sweet, and a little Asian. Serve with toothpicks, and you have a one-bite appetizer that is so very simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 rice vinegar
  • 3 tablesspoons siracha sauce
  1. Mix ketchup, soy sauce, vinegar, sriracha, brown sugar, sesame oil, ginger, and garlic in a saucepan and bring to a simmer over low heat. Add franks and heat until warm, about 5 minutes. Serve with toothpicks.
